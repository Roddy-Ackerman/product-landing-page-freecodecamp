The Edge

This game was developed in one week as part of a weekly game jam from the itch.io website. Cross Gen Games is a one man game studio that designed this game from the ground up.

Sneak Peek

You are a snowman head hopping around on the mountainside looking for the rest of your body. Watch out for floating ice monsters that you can throw snowballs at. Be careful of hidden patches of thin ice that make you collapse to your death. Have fun.
